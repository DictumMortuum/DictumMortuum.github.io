This is a list linking to free online official d&d sources. The main tool you have to your disposal is www.d20srd.org, which has most of the Player's Handbook, Dungeon Master's Guide, Monster Manual I, Unearthed Arcana and Expanded Psionics Handbook. In addition visit crystalkeep which has downloadable pdfs with listings of pretty much everything unfortunately most of the content has been taken down. The links below come from the wizard's 3.5 excerpts archive. Each article has a print function so that you use it more easily.
